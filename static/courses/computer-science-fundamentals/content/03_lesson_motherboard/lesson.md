# The Motherboard

The Motherboard is the backbone of almost every electronic device. It responsible for connecting all of the Computer Components and is comprised of a complex, microscopic network of wires. The motherboard does not do any informational processing, but instead is imperative for keeping the components in contact with each other. This is what all of the physical components connect directly to. Think of the motherboard like your blood vessels. Motherboard commonly have a network device built in to them.
