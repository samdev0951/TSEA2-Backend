# Storage (HDDs and SSDs)

The storage is like your long term memory. Storage holds information that the Computer can remember for years (if your storage components are reliable). Your files, software, operating system and all the information that is remembered when you turn your computer off are all stored on a storage device. The most common two storage components are the Hard Disk Drive (HDD) and Solid State Drive (SSD). Hard Disks are mostly being phased out from consumer products as they are slower than Solid State Storage due to the bottleneck of their mechnical components. SSDs often have a longer lifespan than HDDs because have no moving parts and do not rely on mechnical components.
