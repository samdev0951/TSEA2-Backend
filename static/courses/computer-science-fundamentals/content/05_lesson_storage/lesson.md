# Storage (HDDs and SSDs)

The storage is like your long term memory. Storage holds information that the Computer can remember for years (if your storage components are reliable). Your files, software, operating system and all the information that is remembered when you turn your computer off are all stored on a storage device. The most common two storage components are the Hard Disk Drive (HDD) and Solid State Drive (SSD). Hard Disks are mostly being phased out as they are slower than Solid State Storage due to the bottleneck of their mechnical components. However, SSDs are notorious for being less reliable and have a shorter lifespan as they are 100% electronic. A computer can consist of multiple storage devices at a time.
