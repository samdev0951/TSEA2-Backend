# The PSU (Power Supply Unit)

The Power Supply Unit is like your heart. It delivers power to the various components on your computer, namely the motherboard, GPU and storage devices. Without power, your computer cannot turn on! On mobile devices like laptops, the Power Supply Unit is subsituted with a battery which can be recharged and hold power without an active connection.
